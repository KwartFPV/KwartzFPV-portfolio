About Me

Hi, my name is Thom, but in the FPV world, I go by the nickname Kwartz. Based in France, I started flying FPV drones in 2023. I specialize in cinematic as well as freestyle styles, and I absolutely love creating videos with various styles and music. You can check out my projects on YouTube, where I showcase my latest creations. My motto is "Master the air, defy the ground", which reflects my passion and approach to FPV flying. Fell free to contact me in the section contact of the website or in instagram. Enjoy you flight.

Drones

7inch

One of my favorite drones is the Jeno 7-inch. The Jeno is an open-source French frame project that offers various frame options. I use T-Motor F90 1500KV motors. For the electronics, I fly with the SpeedyBee F405 stack, a Caddx Vista with a TrueRC core antenna, a Happymodel EP1 dual TCXO, a Foxeer M10 for GPS, and a Vifly Finder 2 for the buzzer. For the propellers, I use Gemfan 7040-3. And cnhl 2200mah lipo

5inch

This is my most versatile drone, but I primarily use it for freestyle. Equipped with Pacer V3 1950KV 2207 motors, a JeNo 5.1", caddx vista, Ethix P4 props, and speedybee f405, and with cnhl black 1300mah lipo this drone is ideal for freestyle beginners like me.

Cap Higuer

Cap Higuer, a stunning hike on the Spanish side connecting Hendaye to Pasaia, offers beautiful weather and landscapes in shades of blue and green. I managed only three flights with my 7-inch drone, but the footage remains amazing. If you get the chance to go, don’t hesitate.

Review project

Iparla

Ah, Iparla, once again a beautiful place located in the Basque Country near the France-Spain border. This small mountain range is truly the perfect spot to explore long-range flying. It’s where I did my first long-range flights, and I can’t wait to go back.
